Phantasmauris

Phantasmauris is a location-based electroacoustic composition consists of five tracks. Developed as an aural augmented reality platform, audiences can listen to different soundscapes in each suggested location.

Each track is arranged with field recordings and their digitally processed signals based on the correlations between sound, space and listeners. The sounds are recorded in quarantined Rotterdam to explore the shifted sonic quality of the city.

The project combines the psychogeopraphic process of cartography and composition in a form of acousmatic music in the spectromorphological perspective.

Phantasmauris is a project by Hyunji Jung.
Supported by CBK Rotterdam, 2020
